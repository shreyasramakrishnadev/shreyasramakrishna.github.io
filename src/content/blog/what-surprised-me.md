The itinerary-style travel post is the easiest to write and the least
interesting to read. This template pushes toward something better.

## What I expected

One or two sentences on your mental picture going in.

## What actually surprised me

The real content of the post. Could be a place, a food, a conversation,
a logistical thing nobody warns you about.

## What I'd tell a friend going there

The one piece of advice that's actually useful, not "bring comfortable shoes."
